QUARTER 1

What is streaming

Streaming is a type of media that is a process of transmitting or receiving data, especially video and audio material over in an any devices as long it’s capable to use in streaming and as long your network is steady.

How does streaming work?

Just like other data that's sent over the Internet, audio and video data is broken down into data packets. Each packet contains a small piece of the file, and an audio or video player in the browser on the client device takes the flow of data packets and interprets them as video or audio.

Types of Streaming Content

Streaming content have a two types there are recorded streaming and live streaming, which this two type of streaming differ from each other how they are presented and created.

Common streaming content types

Video games, movies, music videos, educational vdeos, podcasts and interviews. In this list Video games are the most popular because Video games bring people together, even when they are apart. Many games now can be played over the internet, connecting friends and family.

Most used platform

The most used platform in streaming content are youtube live among the twitch, facebook live, Instagram live, Streamyard, Dacast, Wirecast and Streamshark.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

A good stream is able to fully capture the audience’s attention. A good stream is respectful and inclusive to their audience, it should not seek to disrespect or alienate a person or group.
